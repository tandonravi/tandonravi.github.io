Research

My research is organized around four connected themes: privacy and security, trustworthy AI/ML, efficient AI systems, and information theory. Across these themes, I am interested in developing mathematical foundations, algorithms, and practical systems for AI that are reliable, private, efficient, and grounded in fundamental limits.

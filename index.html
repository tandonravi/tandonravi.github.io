Biography: I am the Litton Industries John M. Leonis Distinguished Associate Professor in the Department of ECE at the University of Arizona. I received the B.Tech. degree in Electrical Engineering from IIT Kanpur in 2004 and the Ph.D. degree in ECE from the University of Maryland, College Park in 2010. From 2010 to 2012, I was a post-doctoral research associate at Princeton University. From 2012-2015, I was a Research Assistant Professor at Virginia Tech. I received an NSF CAREER Award in 2017, the 2018 Keysight Early Career Professor Award, a Best Paper Award at 2011 IEEE Globecom conference and the Craig M. Berge Faculty Fellowship at University of Arizona in 2024.
